About WildScape Zoo

Learn about our mission, history, and commitment to conservation

Our Mission

WildScape Zoo is dedicated to wildlife conservation, education, and providing exceptional care for our animal residents. We strive to create meaningful connections between people and wildlife while promoting conservation efforts worldwide.

Our History

Founded in 1985, WildScape Zoo began as a small wildlife sanctuary with just a few native species. Over the years, we've grown into a world-class zoological park housing over 500 animals across 100 different species, while maintaining our commitment to animal welfare and conservation.

Conservation Efforts

We actively participate in over 30 species survival plans and contribute to conservation projects around the globe. Our team works tirelessly to protect endangered species and their habitats through research, breeding programs, and public education.
