Our Animal Residents

Discover the incredible wildlife at WildScape Zoo

African Lion

Panthera leo

Vulnerable

African lions are social cats that live in prides. Our pride consists of 2 males, 4 females, and their cubs.

Habitat: African Savannah

Diet: Carnivore

Asian Elephant

Elephas maximus

Endangered

Asian elephants are highly intelligent and social animals. Our herd includes 3 females and 1 male.

Habitat: Tropical Forests

Diet: Herbivore

Reticulated Giraffe

Giraffa camelopardalis reticulata

Endangered

Giraffes are the tallest land animals. Our tower includes 2 adults and 1 juvenile.

Habitat: African Savannah

Diet: Herbivore

Giant Panda

Ailuropoda melanoleuca

Vulnerable

Giant pandas are beloved bears native to China. We have a breeding pair as part of the species survival plan.

Habitat: Bamboo Forests

Diet: Herbivore (mostly bamboo)

Emperor Penguin

Aptenodytes forsteri

Near Threatened

Emperor penguins are the tallest and heaviest of all living penguin species.

Habitat: Antarctic

Diet: Carnivore

Komodo Dragon Image

Komodo Dragon

Varanus komodoensis

Endangered

Komodo dragons are the largest living lizards in the world.

Habitat: Indonesian Islands

Diet: Carnivore
